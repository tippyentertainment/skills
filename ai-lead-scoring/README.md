# AI Lead Scoring System

## Overview
AI-powered lead scoring with XGBoost for 3.4x pipeline velocity improvement.

## Features
- Behavioral Scoring: Engagement velocity, content consumption, email engagement
- Demographic Scoring: Company size, industry ICP match, job title
- Firmographic Scoring: Growth signals, tech stack, competitor analysis
- Real-Time API: <100ms latency per prediction
- CRM Integration: Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, Zoho

## Expected Results
- Pipeline Velocity: 3.4x improvement
- Conversion Rate: 2-3x on hot leads (score > 70)
- Sales Efficiency: 50% less time on cold leads
